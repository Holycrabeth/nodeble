## Hiring Plan: None (For Now)

Founder intends to do everything solo, indefinitely. This is viable for Phase 1-2 given:
- Backend is already built (4 production systems)
- AI assistance accelerates frontend development
- User base is small (5-50 users initially)
- No regulatory compliance team needed (pure software tool model)

### When Hiring Might Become Necessary

| Trigger | Role Needed | When |
|---------|------------|------|
| 100+ users, support tickets overwhelming | Part-time customer support | Phase 3+ |
| GUI needs professional polish for marketing | Freelance UI/UX designer (one-off) | Phase 2-3 |
| Multi-broker expansion (5+ brokers) | Backend developer (contract) | Phase 4 |
| Revenue > $10K MRR, ready to scale | First full-time hire (generalist) | Year 2+ |

### Key Principle
> Stay solo as long as possible. Hire only when the pain of NOT hiring exceeds the cost and complexity of hiring. AI tools extend the solo founder's runway significantly.
